# Motivator — Cold Outreach Sequence
**Target:** HR leads & team managers | Companies with 50–500 employees
**Goal:** Book demo calls

---

## 1. Cold Email Sequence

---

### Email 1 — The Opener (Day 0)

**Subject Line A:** `{{first_name}}, quick question about retention`
**Subject Line B:** `Your team's engagement problem`

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

Most {{role}}s at companies like {{company}} tell me the same thing: they've tried pizza parties, bonuses, even ping pong tables — and people still quietly disengage.

The problem isn't motivation. It's that work doesn't feel like it matters day-to-day.

We built Motivator to fix that. Teams using it turn their actual work into something people want to show up for — with achievements, friendly competition, and real rewards.

Would it make sense to show you how it works in 15 minutes?

— [Your Name]

**CTA:** Reply to book a 15-min demo.

---

### Email 2 — The Stat (Day 3)

**Subject Line A:** `62% fewer disengaged employees`
**Subject Line B:** `What happened after 90 days at {{company}}'s competitor`

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

Quick follow-up. Didn't want this to get buried.

One mid-size team (similar headcount to {{company}}) plugged Motivator into their daily workflows. After 90 days:

- 62% drop in disengaged employees
- 2.4x more peer recognition
- Voluntary turnover fell by a third

Here's a 3-minute video showing exactly what their setup looks like: [Demo Video Link]

Worth a look?

— [Your Name]

**CTA:** Watch the 3-min demo video, then reply if it clicks.

---

### Email 3 — The Break-Up (Day 7)

**Subject Line A:** `Should I close your file?`
**Subject Line B:** `Not the right time, {{first_name}}?`

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

I don't want to be that person who keeps emailing into the void.

If engagement isn't a priority for {{company}} right now, totally get it — just reply "not now" and I'll disappear.

But if it is and you just haven't had the time, here's a one-click link to grab 15 minutes on my calendar: [Calendly Link]

Either way, no hard feelings.

— [Your Name]

**CTA:** Reply "not now" or book 15 min via the calendar link.
