# Motivator — Marketing Plan

## Goal
Book 5-10 demo calls with HR leads and team managers at companies with 50-500 employees.

---

## Step 1: Record Demo Video (DONE)

A 60-second screen recording showing the full product: Login → Dashboard → Members → Leaderboard → Game Plan Builder → Challenges → Rewards → Analytics. Text overlays on each feature. No voiceover needed — the visuals speak.

Use this video everywhere: cold emails, LinkedIn DMs, landing page.

---

## Step 2: Find Prospects

### Where to find HR leads and team managers

**Free methods:**

1. **LinkedIn Search** — Search "HR Manager" or "People Operations" or "Team Lead". Filter by company size (51-200 or 201-500 employees). Browse profiles, connect directly.

2. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** (free 30-day trial) — More powerful filters: role, company size, industry, location. Shows exactly who to target.

3. **Company websites** — Visit target company sites, check "About" or "Team" pages for leadership names.

**Paid tools (to get emails):**

1. **Apollo.io** (free tier: 50 emails/month) — Search by role, company size, industry. Returns verified emails + LinkedIn profiles. Best free option.

2. **Hunter.io** (free tier: 25 searches/month) — Enter a company domain, get employee email addresses.

3. **Snov.io** — Similar to Apollo. Has a LinkedIn extension that finds emails directly from profiles.

### Quick start
1. Sign up for Apollo.io (free)
2. Search: "HR Manager" + "51-200 employees" + target industry/location
3. Export 20-30 contacts with emails
4. You're ready for Step 3

## Step 3: Cold Outreach Sequence (7 days)

### Timing

| Touch | Channel    | Day | Action                                      |
|-------|------------|-----|---------------------------------------------|
| 1     | Email      | 0   | Send Email 1 (The Opener)                   |
| 2     | LinkedIn   | 1   | Send connection request                     |
| 3     | Email      | 3   | Send Email 2 (The Stat + demo video link)   |
| 4     | LinkedIn DM| 4   | Send follow-up DM (if connection accepted)  |
| 5     | Email      | 7   | Send Email 3 (The Break-Up)                 |

### Email 1 — The Opener (Day 0)

**Subject Line A:** `{{first_name}}, quick question about retention`
**Subject Line B:** `Your team's engagement problem`

Hi {{first_name}},

Most {{role}}s at companies like {{company}} tell me the same thing: they've tried pizza parties, bonuses, even ping pong tables — and people still quietly disengage.

The problem isn't motivation. It's that work doesn't feel like it matters day-to-day.

We built Motivator to fix that. Teams using it turn their actual work into something people want to show up for — with achievements, friendly competition, and real rewards.

Would it make sense to show you how it works in 15 minutes?

— [Your Name]

## Step 4: Landing Page

Create a simple one-page site with:
- Headline: "Turn Work Into a Game Your Team Wants to Play"
- Embedded demo video
- 3 outcome bullets (not features):
  - "62% fewer disengaged employees"
  - "2.4x more peer recognition"
  - "Turnover drops within 90 days"
- Calendly embed for "Book a Demo"
- Company logo, dark theme matching the product

Use this URL in all outreach instead of raw Railway links.

---

## Step 5: Measure & Iterate

Track these metrics weekly:
- Emails sent
- Open rate (aim for 40%+)
- Reply rate (aim for 5%+)
- Demos booked
- LinkedIn connection acceptance rate

After 5 demo calls, review:
- Which pain points resonated most?
- What objections came up?
- Which company size / industry converted best?

Use learnings to update messaging and targeting for the next batch.
